How Vate Works

1.  Create Your Project

Our custom, step-by-step process will enable you to enter the details of your next home remodel into our user friendly interface. To get started, all you need to do is answer a few basic questions about the specifics of your project. Then you can post pictures, video explanations, and even provide links to your favorite inspirational images from around the web.

2.  Post Your Project

Your project specifications are viewed by local professionals that are competing to work on your renovation. Because we care about your privacy, we never share your home address or contact info. Keep the project public to let all the contractors in your area see your project or make it private and only the contractors you select will be able to see your project and provide estimates.

3.  Get Estimates

Vate is different. We don't cherry pick which professionals you should work with or limit your options in any way. Instead, you are presented with all of your estimates in a user friendly format that lets you choose the right professional for your renovation.

FAQs

How much does it cost to post my project on Vate?

Nothing. You pay nothing.

‍It's completely free to post your projects, upload your videos and images, and receive estimates from contractors.

Who can see my project pictures, videos, and other info?

It's totally up to you.

‍There are two different ways to post your project on Vate: contractors only, or privately. You choose which option is best for you when you post your project.

If you set your project to contractors only, only contractors will be able to view your project details. Other homeowners on Vate won't be able to see it.

If you set your project to private, no one can see it except for you and the contractors you invite to estimate it.

What info do contractors see about me and what info do you share with them?

We only share what info you let them see.

‍Whatever information you post in your project is what contractors can see. They see what you see. We don't share your phone number, street address, or email address with anyone. We don't even ask you for your home address when you signup. You can use Vate to provide a contact number or email address to a contractor after you've gotten an estimate, but only if you want to. For your privacy, we also recommend you don't share your street address or contact info in any images, videos, or written descriptions you post on Vate.

What if I want more estimates on my project?

Easy. You can invite more contractors to estimate on your project.

‍There are two ways to get estimates from contractors on Vate: Sit back and wait for contractors to find your project and give you estimates;

OR

find contractors you like in our directory and invite them to estimate your project.

What do I do once I've received a few estimates?

You can reach out to any contractor you like using their contact information, which can be found on their company profile.

Can contractors see estimates from other contractors?

No.

‍As the project owner, you're the only one who can see all the estimates posted on your project. Contractors can only see their own estimates. The goal of Vate is to get you accurate estimates for your home renovation without any hassle on your end. However, we're not trying to turn your projects into a low price auction.
